What the course covers

The course studies 10 propositions from the Minimalist Wisdom Text. These propositions concern being, necessary reality, possible reality, substances, the monad, the soul or node, knowledge, unity, and community.

Robert often uses the word “hypothesis” when discussing these propositions. This reflects an intellectually responsible approach: the ideas are treated seriously and practiced with confidence, while still acknowledging the difference between one’s present understanding and the fullness of truth.

The course is especially suitable for independent learners, spiritually serious students, and philosophically inclined viewers who want a compact framework for contemplating reality as a whole.
