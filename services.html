Services

Claystone Studies offers one-on-one tutoring and coaching sessions over Zoom video or Zoom audio. (You may select video or audio when you book the session.) Below are the one-on-one session types currently offered. All sessions are presently with Robert Davis.

(1) Philosophy Tutoring and Coaching. One hour session: $135. Robert Davis works with you on a philosophy topic, helping you to more deeply understand it. In these sessions, Robert seeks to help his clients attain philosophical wisdom. One optional method in these sessions, depending on the nature and preferences of the client, is to explore the philosophy topic using the dialectical method, also known as the Socratic method, which here means using inquiry and questioning to go deeper into the material and gain more understanding.

(2) Writing and Language Tutoring. One hour session: $65. This session type encompasses areas such as general writing, creative writing, language, communication, and speech and debate. This session type is useful for students who wish to improve their reading and writing skills.
