Astrology

Astrology is about the interconnected nature of energies in a system. Robert works with sidereal astrology, intuitively resonating with the objective quality of the sidereal system, in which the meaning of the zodiac signs remains the same for northern and southern hemispheres, and where the vital essence of each sign is predominantly determined by the stars rather than the seasons. Another note: since we cannot independently verify, for example, that a particular zodiac sign accurately signifies that portion of the zodiac (in the way that we can verify with independent certitude that 5 + 6 = 11), our understanding in astrology seems to be based more on systemic tradition and intuition, rather than scientifically demonstrable certitude. In astrology, we have inherited a tradition from those that came before us: this is evident with the data of the zodiac signs and nakshatras, emerging from ancient Babylonia, India, and perhaps other cultures.
