Services

Claystone Studies offers one-on-one tutoring and coaching sessions over Zoom video or Zoom audio. (You may select video or audio when you book the session.) Below are the one-on-one session types currently offered. All sessions are presently with Robert Davis.

(1) Spiritual Coaching. Robert offers his guidance and insights for your spiritual path and practice. He shares from the progress he has made on his own path. Robert seeks to help his clients attain knowledge and vitality, the same goals he is working toward, as well. It may be noted that Robert is a teacher and spiritual coach, but not an infallible guru: like every individual, he has positive and negative qualities and is working on a path of his own. (Session packages may be booked here.)
Book Spiritual Coaching Session

(2) Philosophy Tutoring. Robert works with you on a philosophy topic, helping you to more deeply understand it. In these sessions, Robert seeks to help his clients attain philosophical wisdom. One optional method in these sessions, depending on the nature and preferences of the client, is to explore the philosophy topic using the dialectical method, also known as the Socratic method, which here means using questions and answers, back and forth, to go deeper into the material and gain more understanding. (Session packages may be booked here.)

(3) Open Discussion. This session type is for the purpose of having a discussion of a topic of your choice with Robert.
