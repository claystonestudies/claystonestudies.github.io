Life Coaching Session

1 hour on Zoom

In our lives, there are several endpoints that we are all seeking to move towards. We all wish to enter into greater states of knowledge, preferring to be in states of knowledge rather than in states of ignorance. Secondly, we seek to increase the level of harmony and unity in our beings, so that our nature and presence in this world may be characterized by coherence, balance, and centeredness. Third, we are all seeking to enter into deeper states of vitality. Vitality, which means life, is essentially the essence of what we call happiness. The experience of happiness is the presence of genuine life encountered by us in our hearts. We also seek to move towards a sense of infinity, where we can touch the experience that regardless of any limitations we may encounter, there is always a solution, and there is always an overarching harmony. In my life coaching service, I intend to contribute to your ability to move towards these goals. We can begin from wherever you happen to stand in life, with your difficulties, successes, strengths, and weaknesses. My intention is to contribute to the harmony of your path and journey, so that after you emerge from working with me, you feel that you are in a better place.
