About Claystone Studies

Hi there! My name is Robert. I created Claystone Studies in 2025 as a space to jointly pursue knowledge and collaborate in the sciences, humanities, and philosophy. I have a personal interest in learning and philosophy and seek to explore and grow. I graduated Cornell University with a B.A. in philosophy. Before starting Claystone Studies, I offered tutoring services on Varsity Tutors and Wyzant. Here at Claystone Studies, I offer tutoring services and one-on-one sessions over Zoom. You can view my video and content on YouTube and X. I also write books.
