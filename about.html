About Claystone Studies

Claystone Studies values the existence of a community space of discourse, in which the sharing of ideas, learning, and understanding may take place with respect and tranquility. When multiple perspectives harmoniously interact with each other, new insights may emerge. The act of studying is a unique and special act, since it signifies the understanding, of an individual or a group of individuals, approaching more alignment with the structure of reality as a whole, through this interaction with one of its aspects. Since all actions proceed from the content and quality of our understanding, the act of the optimization of our intelligence is highly significant in human life, even occupying a principal ethical dimension. Mathematics gives us an example of unchanging truths, and this principle of eternal truth can serve as a strong foundation during periods of dynamic change, movement, and flow. By increasing our connection to the essential structures of being and the universe in this way, vitality can further pulsate in our hearts, and we can approach states that are self-evidently choiceworthy, full of insight, luminosity, care, love, and tranquility.

About Robert Davis

Robert Davis started Claystone Studies for the purpose of participating in the edifying activities of knowledge, service, communication, learning, and community growth. From youth, Robert had an interest in truth and wisdom, working with these themes in various ways. At one point, he exited his formal education process for some years in order to spend more time in meditation and spiritual inquiry. Robert received a B.A. in Philosophy from Cornell University. In general, Robert values the health of the environment, planetary harmony, and a sustainable, healthy future for humanity. In his perspective, vitality is principally emergent in the life of a community, in which individuals are contributing parts, in harmony with each other and the whole.

Robert offers video material on his YouTube channel.
