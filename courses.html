Minimalist Wisdom Text Course: A Study in Metaphysics
Topics: Ontology, Metaphysics
In this course in ontology and metaphysics, Robert Davis presents and discusses a compact—hence the name minimalist—wisdom text in metaphysics that he authored. The text presents a useful framework for conceiving the nature of foundational being and reality, central topics in metaphysics and ontology.

Eight Universal Cosmic Principles Course
Topics: Ontology, Metaphysics
In this course in ontology and metaphysics, Robert Davis discusses, with extensive commentary, eight universal, cosmic principles pertaining to the ultimate structure of reality and experience in all of space and time. These principles contribute to a rejuvenation and clarification of the human worldview as we proceed to the end of the 21st century and beyond.
