Course 1

A study in metaphysics

Minimalist Wisdom Course

This course examines the Minimalist Wisdom Text, a concise text in metaphysics and ontology. The text contains 10 propositions, and the course studies them slowly, with one video devoted to each proposition, along with an introduction and a review and conclusion.

The course is intended for students who want a careful way to think about being, possible and necessary reality, substances, the monad, the soul or node, knowledge, unity, and community. Its style is contemplative and direct, presenting philosophical ideas in language meant to support understanding rather than overwhelm the student.

Robert often uses the word "hypothesis" when discussing these propositions. This reflects an intellectually responsible approach: the ideas are treated seriously and practiced with confidence, while still acknowledging the difference between one's present understanding and the fullness of truth.

What you receive

  • 12 videos
  • About 3.7 hours of video content
  • 1 PDF: the Minimalist Wisdom Text
  • 10 proposition lessons
  • Introduction, review, and conclusion videos

Good fit for

Students interested in metaphysics, spiritual foundations, self-knowledge, the relation between logic and being, and a careful universal language for contemplating reality.

Course 2

Eight universal principles

Eight Cosmic Principles Course

This course presents eight universal cosmic principles that Robert Davis has organized as a concise framework for contemplating reality. The principles are discussed in a natural and steady way, with one video for each principle, plus an introduction and a summary and conclusion.

The principles concern unity, cause and effect, community, action reciprocity, nodal life, intelligibility, epistemic limits, and the dependence of disunity on unity. Together, they offer a compact structure for students who want to think more clearly about metaphysics, knowledge, ethics, and the universe as a whole.

The course does not depend on a long textbook. Its PDF gives the title and definition of each principle, while the videos provide the fuller explanation and contemplative context.

What you receive

  • 10 videos
  • 2 hours 48 minutes of video content
  • 1 PDF with the eight cosmic principles
  • Eight principle lessons
  • Introduction, summary, and conclusion videos

Good fit for

Students seeking a shorter course centered on a compact framework for organizing metaphysical, ethical, and contemplative understanding.
