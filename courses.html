Courses

Minimalist Wisdom Text Course: A Study in Metaphysics and Spirituality
In this course, Robert Davis presents and discusses a compact—hence the name minimalist—wisdom text in metaphysics that he authored. The text presents an extraordinarily useful framework for understanding reality that would be appreciated by both the philosopher and the spiritual aspirant. Robert uses this text in his own studies and practice. The information and insights he presents in this course are genuine and tested by the fire of authentic labor.

Eight Universal Cosmic Principles Course
In this course, Robert Davis presents eight universal, cosmic principles pertaining to the ultimate structure of reality and experience in all of space and time. These principles contribute to a rejuvenation of humanity's worldview as we proceed to the end of the 21st century and beyond.
